Our Story

Born out of the need to empower intro cooks, dishedUp was created to make cooking fun and accessible. Our team—Sabrina, Khushy, and Ekam—designed an app that gamifies your culinary journey by providing interactive quests, personalized tutorials, and even voice-enabled navigation.

Whether you’re a busy professional, an overworked student, or a multitasking parent, our app helps you organize weekly healthy meals and grocery plans, gain confidence in the kitchen, and explore new recipes with a twist.
