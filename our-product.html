dishedUp

dishedUp is an interactive cooking game aimed to help individuals meet their dietary and food goals. Users can practise new cuisines, experiment with different ingredients, and track their progress toward healthier eating habits through progress tracking features.

Gameplay

Dive into a dynamic, interactive cooking experience that turns learning into a game. Complete challenges, master lessons through hands-free interaction, and track your progress as you become a culinary pro.

Sign Up Process

Sign up for an account on DishedUp and input the cuisine you want to challenge! Add in your personal dietary restrictions and allergies to be given unique recipes catered to you!

Lesson Taking

Engage in step-by-step interactive lessons that guide you through each cooking process. Adjust the pace using voice commands and revisit sections as needed.

Other Features

Complete challenges and recipes to gain points to compete against your friends in the leaderboard. Track your progress in different cuisines on the profile page and see your favourited recipes!
